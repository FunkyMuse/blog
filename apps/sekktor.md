Using your API keys, this application allows you to explore data gathered by Shodan.io, censys.io, zoomeye.org in order to discover exposed services all over the Internet.
Search for specific terms or any popular queries and start browsing connected devices.

This app is made for security analysis to find and explore whether your online device is vulnerable and already present on the internet.

The data presented in this app belongs to
https://www.shodan.io/
https://www.zoomeye.org/
https://censys.io/

and their respectful owners, if you feel that you've been exposed somehow, contact one of these sites.

Use this data for pen-testing and malware analysis for your internet devices.
Security plays a big part in all of your applications.
